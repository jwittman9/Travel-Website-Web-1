A trip with the wandering Traveler
Hi. My name is Lisa and I will be telling you about an adventure I had with the Wandering Traveler. I chose the italy package and at first I was skeptical but now I'm a believer. At first I was a bit concerned with the price but after seeing the excellent service and how they really took care of everything I think it was more than worth it. I chose Italy and it was amazing, so sit back, relax and I will show you everything my package offered.

Day 1
First we woke up early and visited the Colosseum. Then after my guide named Richard took us to lunch we saw the famous ancient pool of the gods. The tour guide knew where all the best landmarks were including the statue called the skirmish of Hercules and finally we saw the dome of heaven.

Day 2
On the second day after going through our schedule Richard took us to the famous stairs of swirling. We ate a wonderful lunch and saw many great works of art including the virgin mary's woe. Finally he flew us over the city and we took it all in.

Day 3
On the third day we went and saw the pristine chapel. Then we saw a play and gazed upon the statue of David, one of my bucket list items. We had lunch in the gardens of Italy and posed in front of the leaning tower of Pisa.

Day 4
Finally on the last day we went to Mergo's museum of priceless art. I always wanted to go here and had no idea it was so spectacular in person. I saw the famous mural of the angel Inci making a displeased face. I was anything but displeased however. This vacation of a lifetime was coming to a close but I already have my next one planned. We enjoyed one last sunset and dinner, told Richard goodbye and got on the plane for home. I will be sure to book a trip with the Wandering Traveler again and I already requested Richard to be our guide.
