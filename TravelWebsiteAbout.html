The Wandering Traveler

We are a prepackaged, no-worries travel company. Simply name the country, a few activities and locations and we will take care of the rest! We will provide a guide who will take you all around any one of the 1507 fabulous locations we visit. For one small fee we will take you on the adventure of a lifetime. Don't know where you want to go? Simply name any country worldwide and we will take care of the rest. Our highly trained guides know all the hotspots, what to do, where to go and most importantly where you can relax and unwind! We will take care of all your travel and satisfaction is guaranteed or you won't pay a cent. We take all instructions ranging from complex to simple. You could tell us you want to go to Spain and see your top 10 bucket list destinations along with a certain hotel and a few restaurants or you could simply tell us you want to visit a beach. We take care of all the rest!
 Our founder James Bartholomew Wittman the third lived by a simple motto: "There is no greater pleasure in life than to travel safely without a worry in the world" We have taken this saying to heart and have done so since 1705. Our goal is no less than at least one experience of a lifetime per customer. We pledge we shall always put the customer first and ensure an enjoyable experience at a reasonable price.
